## Overview

Led the product vision and execution for a consumer-friendly DeFi savings application. Architected the system design and coordinated cross-functional teams from concept to launch.

Kinsu simplified yield farming for mainstream users, achieving $10M+ TVL at peak with 5,000+ active users.

## Key Features

### Automated Yield Optimization
- Real-time yield comparison across 20+ protocols
- Automated fund allocation to highest APY
- Gas-optimized rebalancing
- Compound interest reinvestment
- Tax-loss harvesting

### Multi-Chain Portfolio Management
- Support for Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism
- Cross-chain yield aggregation
- Unified portfolio dashboard
- Bridge integration for seamless transfers
- Chain-specific gas optimization

### Risk Assessment Dashboard
- Protocol safety scores
- Historical APY trends
- Impermanent loss calculations
- Smart contract audit status
- Insurance coverage indicators

### One-Click Diversification Strategies
- Conservative (stablecoins, blue-chip DeFi)
- Moderate (mixed stables + ETH)
- Aggressive (high-yield farms, new protocols)
- Custom allocation builder
- Rebalancing automation

### Mobile-First Responsive Design
- Progressive web app (PWA)
- Touch-optimized interfaces
- Offline transaction queuing
- Biometric authentication
- Push notifications for opportunities

## Product Strategy

### Target Audience
- Crypto-native users tired of manual yield farming
- Traditional finance users exploring DeFi
- Small to mid-size portfolios ($1K-$100K)
- Risk-conscious yield seekers

### Value Proposition
- **Save time**: Automated vs 10+ hours/week manual farming
- **Maximize yield**: Algorithm beats manual by avg 3.2%
- **Reduce risk**: Diversification + safety scoring
- **Simplify complexity**: One-click vs multi-protocol juggling

1. **Simplicity wins**: Every additional click costs 10% conversion
2. **Trust is everything**: Audits and transparency > features
